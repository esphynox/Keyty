# Code of Conduct

Keyty is an open-source project. Participation should be respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of other contributors and users.
- Assume good intent, but be clear and factual when disagreeing.
- Keep discussions relevant to the issue, pull request, or project goal.
- Give actionable feedback and avoid personal attacks.
- Respect user privacy, especially when sharing screenshots, logs, or recordings that may include input activity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing sensitive input data, logs, or recordings from other people without consent.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block users when behavior is harmful to the project or community.

To report conduct concerns, email support@keyty.app.
